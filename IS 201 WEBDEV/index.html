Folsom, California

Distinctive by nature since 1946

Top 5 things to do in Folsom:

  1. Visit the Harris Center for the Arts
  2. Shop at Palladio Mall
    • H&M
    • Nordstrom Rack
    • Palladio 16 Cinema
    • Dave and Buster's
    • Johnny Rocket's
    • And much more!
  3. See the tigers at the Folsom City Zoo
  4. Hike the Johnny Cash Memorial Trail
  5. Watch a play at the Sutter Street Theatre

Why live in Folsom?

  • One of the lowest crime rates in Sacramento county and in all of California (see visual below)
  • Unaffected by recent California fires
  • Award-winning schools
  • Proximity to both ski resorts (40 min) and beaches (1.5 hrs)
  • Great for families: youth sports and clubs for all ages
